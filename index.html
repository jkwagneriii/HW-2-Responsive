About Me

Before I started my coding career at Northwestern, I was working for Intelligentsia Coffee. I was a barista at the Logan Square location and created last years seasonal drinks for the national menus. During that time, I was running two comedy shows every week with a group called LIAR. Before that, I was a bartender at The Aviary from The Alinea Group.

Before THAT I was getting my BFA in Acting from Millikin University and got to be in a movie or two. Outside of all that, time is best spent with my love Heather and our two cats Yuzu and Nari. We also brew kombucha - very legit.
